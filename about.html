Our Journey

The leading Conversational Relationship Platform

We established Data Analytics Hub for businesses that align with our commitment to exceptional customer service. For many, superior customer support is not just an operational function—it’s a strategic advantage and a fundamental element of brand identity.

However, in 2008, there were no customer service platforms that truly embodied our customer-centric principles. Existing help desks often treated customers as mere statistics and hindered the development of meaningful, long-lasting relationships. Motivated to bring about change, we embarked on our journey.
